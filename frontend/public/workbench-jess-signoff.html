1 · Your color language — confirmed
These are the colors you already use in your Load Count sheet. We're going to use the same ones in the app so nothing new to learn.
● Green

Auto-validated · awaiting your confirm

100% matched load with a photo attached. The matcher thinks it's clean. You still have to click Confirm before it moves to ready-to-build.

● Yellow

Ready to build · in the dispatch queue

You confirmed it. Steph, Scout, or Keli can now grab it and build it in PCS.

● Pink

Built / pushed · completed

Typed into PCS (or auto-pushed, post-OAuth). Done from the build side; Katie's verification begins here.

● Orange

Needs rate / facility info

The matcher knows the weight but not the rate. Goes to the Needs Rate sub-tab.

● Purple

Missing ticket / not arrived

Ticket number not yet on file or load isn't showing as delivered. Goes to the Missing Ticket sub-tab.

● Cyan

Missing driver

Driver field is blank or doesn't match a known driver. Goes to the Missing Driver sub-tab.

"I do like the tiers and percentages for matched loads. 100% matched load (have to have a photo attached). These can auto turn yellow (they are currently green) and go to the ready to build tab under the dispatch desk." — Jess, 2026-04-17
